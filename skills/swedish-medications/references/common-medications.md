## Abbreviations

- **OTC**: Over-the-counter (receptfritt)
- **Rx**: Prescription required (receptbelagt)
- **SSRI**: Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor
- **PPI**: Proton pump inhibitor
- **ASA**: Acetylsalicylic acid (aspirin)
